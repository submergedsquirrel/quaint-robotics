Welcome to Michigan Birdwatching, your go-to resource for exploring the rich birdlife of the Great Lakes State. Whether you're a seasoned birder or just starting out, Michigan offers a variety of stunning habitats, from the dense forests of the Upper Peninsula to the serene wetlands of the lower state. Discover the beauty of over 450 bird species that call Michigan home throughout the year.

Join us as we guide you to some of Michigan’s best birdwatching spots, from the shores of Lake Huron to the breathtaking wilderness of Isle Royale. Learn about migratory patterns, seasonal highlights, and local hotspots where you can witness everything from vibrant warblers to majestic bald eagles. With our detailed maps and birding tips, you'll be ready for an unforgettable outdoor adventure.

At Michigan Birdwatching, we believe in fostering a deep connection with nature through birding. Stay updated with our events, workshops, and birdwatching tours led by experts. Whether you're looking for quiet moments in nature or to connect with a passionate birdwatching community, we’re here to help you take flight on your birding journey.
